Ramen

This is a recipe for making spicy ramen.

Ingredients

  • Ramen
  • Spicies
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Pour spicies into ramen
  2. Pour water in up to fill line in ramen
  3. Put in microwave for 3 minutes
  4. Let sit for one minute
  5. Pour out water
  6. Put in sauce and mix it together
